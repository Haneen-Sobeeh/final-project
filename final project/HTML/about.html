About Our Project

Our project aims to provide university students living in dormitories with affordable, high-quality home-cooked meals. We understand the need for nutritious and delicious food options that fit a student budget, so we partner with local cooks to deliver freshly prepared meals right to their doorsteps. By offering traditional and healthy dishes made from quality ingredients, we make it easy for students to enjoy wholesome meals that remind them of home, supporting their busy lifestyles while fostering a sense of community.

Frequently Asked Questions

FAQs

What is Home Chef?

Home Chef is a platform that connects university students living in dorms with local cooks who provide nutritious, affordable, and home-cooked meals delivered right to their doorsteps.

How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse through the menu, select your preferred dish, and follow the checkout process. Your meal will be prepared fresh and delivered to your dorm.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and mobile payment options for your convenience.

Can I customize my meals?

Yes, you can customize your meals by specifying dietary preferences and additional instructions when placing your order.
